FAQ

A few quick answers

What is bysloth?

bysloth is a planner for overland travel. Give it two cities and it maps a realistic route between them using trains, buses, ferries, and shared taxis — no flights — with a confidence rating on every leg.

Is bysloth free?

Yes — bysloth is free during its current private beta, with no paid plans.

Does bysloth book tickets?

No — bysloth plans the route and links out to the operator for each leg. It doesn't process payments itself.
