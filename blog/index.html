Field notes

Route guides, slowly written.

The bysloth blog is a collection of long-form overland travel guides — border crossings, bus and train routes, ferry connections, and comparisons with other trip-planning tools — for people planning trips without flights.

Written by the bysloth team, drawing on real routes planned in the app and public information from operators and other travel resources.

Last updated 2026-07-23 · Written by the bysloth team

What will I find in this blog?

Two kinds of posts: route-specific guides (border logistics, transport options, timing) and comparisons against other route-planning tools like Rome2rio and Omio, based on public reviews and our own testing. Every post is dated and updated when the underlying information changes.
