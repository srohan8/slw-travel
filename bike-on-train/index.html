About this data

Policies are sourced from official operator T&Cs and verified by date. Bike policies change frequently — seasonal routes, new rolling stock, and changing regulations all affect what's permitted. Always confirm with the operator before travel, especially for international trips.

Entries marked "Check locally" mean the policy is real but variable — time of day, specific train, or route affects whether you and your bike get on. This is not a "no" — it means ring ahead.

This database is curated and updated manually. Community reports from By Sloth users who've done routes with bikes are layered on top where available.
