Pricing

bysloth is free during its private beta

bysloth has one plan right now: Free, $0/month, no paid tiers. The product is in private beta (v0.4) and every feature — route planning, time estimates, CO₂ savings, bike-on-train lookups, and trip journaling — is included at no cost.

bysloth doesn't sell tickets, so there's no booking fee or commission built into any future pricing plan either — it links out to the operator or booking site for each leg.

Will bysloth stay free forever?

Unknown — we haven't announced a paid plan. If that changes, this page will be updated with the actual terms rather than a placeholder.

Is there a free trial I need to cancel?

No. There's nothing to sign up for beyond a free account, and nothing to cancel — the whole product is free right now.
